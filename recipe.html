Chicken Shashlaik

Chicken Shashliak is basically like a shish kebab, which translates to skewered meat, and is a dish that was popular in central and South Asia. Shish were generally made using chunks of lamb, but chicken kebabs have become just as popular. This has a slight Chinese-style twist to the marinade with the use of soy. This dish, it's all about balancing the sweet, sour, salt, and chili flavors.
